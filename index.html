Meet Bianca Borrello

Hi, I’m Bianca, a psychotherapist and counsellor based in Manchester. Growing up across the Middle East, I was immersed in vibrant, multicultural communities, which have profoundly shaped my perspective and approach to therapy.

My journey into this profession was inspired by my own childhood experiences and the life-changing support I received through therapy as an adult. I am passionate about helping others and have extensive experience working with adults, teenagers, and young people to navigate life’s challenges. Supporting clients on their journey towards growth, healing, and self-discovery is at the heart of what I do.
